Privacy Policy

FocusFlow – Focus Timer & Habit Tracker  ·  Last updated: July 7, 2026
✅ No data collected. No accounts. No tracking.

The short version

FocusFlow does not collect, store, or share any personal information. Everything you do in the app stays on your device. We have no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

Information we collect

We collect no personal information. FocusFlow does not require you to create an account or provide any personal details to use the app.

Data stored on your device

FocusFlow stores the following information locally on your device using your browser's localStorage:

  • Timer settings and session history
  • Habit names and completion logs
  • Coin balance and shop purchases
  • Color theme preferences
  • Achievement progress

This data never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to any server, and we have no ability to access it.

Data sharing

We do not share any data with third parties because we do not collect any data in the first place.

Analytics and tracking

FocusFlow contains no analytics, no tracking software, no advertising SDKs, and no third-party code that monitors your usage.

Advertising

FocusFlow contains no advertisements.

Children's privacy

FocusFlow does not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

Permissions

FocusFlow may request the following device permissions:

  • Internet access — only used to load the app when opened via browser. Not used to transmit data.
  • Storage — used to save your settings and progress locally on your device.

Data deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by going to Settings → Reset all data within the app, or by clearing your browser/app storage in your device settings.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change this privacy policy, we will update the date at the top of this page. We will never introduce data collection without clearly notifying users.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, contact us at: tgubish@gmail.com

This privacy policy applies to the FocusFlow app available on Google Play and at tgubish.github.io/focusflow.
